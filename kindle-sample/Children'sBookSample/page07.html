V
for VICUGNA; on mountains it
    lives,
And soft silky wool for the use of
    Man gives.
W
for WALRUS; in cold Polar Seas,
It swims midst the icebergs with
    safety and case.
X
’XTINCT ANIMAL!—You won’t
    regret,
That creatures like this one no
    longer are met.
Y
is for YAK, the wild ox of Thibet;
The Tartars are glad its long soft
    fleece to get.
Z
is for ZEBRA—the brave desert
    steed,
Who flies o’er the African plains
    at full speed.
