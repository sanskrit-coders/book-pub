I
is for IBEX; a goat with long
    horns,
He lives on the mountains; the
    low lands he scorns.
J
is for JAGUAR, a fierce beast of
    prey,
Who hides in American forests by
    day.
K
is for KANGAROO: upright it
    keeps,
Except when it jumps o’er the
    ground in great leaps.
L
stands for LYNX, clothed in fur
    thick and good,
Which finds home and food in
    the depths of the wood.
