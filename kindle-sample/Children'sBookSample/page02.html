E
is for ELEPHANT, mighty in
    size
But kind to all children, and gentle
    and wise.
F
is for FOX, who lives down under
    ground,
And often is chased by the huntsman
    and hound.
G
is for GOAT,—o’er the Welsh
    hills it flies,
And very rich milk to the children
    supplies.
H
is for HORSE,—one of man’s
    greatest friends:
On our wants and our pleasures
    he gladly attends.
