R
for RHINOCEROS, thick-skinned
    and strong,
To which two great horns on his
    hard nose belong.
S
is for SQUIRREL, who sits on a
    tree;
When cracking his nuts very happy is
    he.
T
for the Tiger, most savage of
    beasts,
Who on all living creatures most
    readily feasts.
U
is for UNICORN,—well known in
    fable;
To find him alive I think no one
    is able.
